# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

\- Never share your OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone    
\- Do not approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps    
\- Always verify the identity of the person before making transactions    
\- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication    
\- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages    
\- Install apps only from official app stores    
\- Regularly check your bank transaction history  

##  UPI / Financial Safety:

\- You do not need to enter a PIN to receive money    
\- Always double-check UPI ID and recipient name before sending money

## Reporting:

\- Report cyber fraud at https://cybercrime.gov.in    
\- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
